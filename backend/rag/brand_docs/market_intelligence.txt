MARKET INTELLIGENCE - Southeast Asia Footwear Distribution

MARKET SIZE: SEA footwear market valued at $8.2B in 2024, growing at 6.8% CAGR.
KEY TRENDS:
  - Casualisation driving demand away from formal toward sneakers and flats
  - E-commerce penetration rising but physical retail still dominant (65% of sales)
  - Mid-market segment ($30-80 USD) fastest growing
  - Sustainability messaging increasingly important to consumers under 30

SINGAPORE MARKET:
  - Mature, highly competitive
  - Strong distributor network already established
  - Good as regional HQ and test market
  - Key locations: Orchard, Bugis, Tampines, Jurong, Woodlands

DISTRIBUTOR LANDSCAPE:
  - Large distributors (10+ brands): Hard to get attention, slow decision making
  - Mid-market SMEs: Best fit — hungry for new brands, faster onboarding
  - Pure sports distributors: Poor fit for StepForward's lifestyle positioning
  - Multi-category footwear distributors: Ideal fit

COMPETITIVE MARGINS IN MARKET:
  - Nike / Adidas: 12-18% distributor margin
  - Clarks / Skechers: 22-28% distributor margin
  - Paragon / Bata: 35-42% distributor margin (value segment)
  - StepForward target: 25-40% (premium-mid, strong value proposition vs sports giants)
