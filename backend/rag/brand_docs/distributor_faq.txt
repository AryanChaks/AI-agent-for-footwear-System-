DISTRIBUTOR FAQ - StepForward Footwear

Q: What territories are available?
A: Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, Philippines, India (select cities), UAE.

Q: What support do distributors get?
A: Dedicated account manager, seasonal lookbooks, digital marketing assets, training on product range, and co-op advertising budget for top-tier partners.

Q: How are payments structured?
A: 30% upfront on first order, net-60 for established partners. LC accepted for large orders.

Q: What is the return policy?
A: 5% return allowance on defective goods. No returns on slow-moving stock.

Q: Are exclusive territories available?
A: Yes, for distributors committing to minimum annual purchase of $250,000.

Q: What is the lead time on orders?
A: 45-60 days from order confirmation for standard SKUs. 30 days for reorders of existing stock.

Q: Do you attend trade shows?
A: Yes — Asia Shoe Fair, Expo Riva Schuh, and regional B2B trade events. Distributors can co-exhibit.

Q: What makes StepForward different from Paragon or Bata?
A: Higher brand equity, stronger digital presence, more fashionable design language, and better margin structure for distributors.
