BRAND: StepForward Footwear
POSITIONING: Premium-to-mid-market footwear brand focused on everyday comfort and urban lifestyle.
TARGET CONSUMER: 18-40 urban professionals and students seeking stylish, comfortable footwear.
PRODUCT CATEGORIES: Sneakers, Casual Shoes, Flats, Sandals, Loafers, Light Formal.
PRICE RANGE: ₹1,500 - ₹8,000 (retail). Mid-premium segment.
TARGET DISTRIBUTOR MARGIN: 25-40%. We offer better margins than sports giants like Nike/Adidas (15%).
EXPANSION REGIONS: Southeast Asia (primary), South Asia, Middle East (secondary).
MINIMUM ORDER QUANTITY: 500 pairs per SKU per season.
IDEAL DISTRIBUTOR PROFILE:
  - Established presence in target region (3+ years)
  - Existing multi-brand portfolio (shows market knowledge)
  - Strong in lifestyle, casual, or comfort categories
  - Mid-market or SME company size preferred for agility
  - Revenue range: $100k - $2M annual footwear sales
  - Stock management capability: avg stock > 50 units
DISQUALIFIERS:
  - Pure sports/performance focus only (poor category fit)
  - Less than 2 years in market
  - Stock levels consistently below 20 units (poor logistics)
KEY SELLING POINTS TO DISTRIBUTORS:
  - Higher margins than global sports brands (25-40% vs 15%)
  - Growing urban consumer demand in Southeast Asia
  - Flexible MOQ for new partners
  - Co-marketing support and seasonal campaigns
  - Exclusive territory agreements available
OBJECTION HANDLING:
  - "We already carry Nike/Adidas": Complement, not compete. Different margin, different consumer.
  - "Unknown brand": Growing brand with strong digital presence and influencer backing.
  - "MOQ too high": Negotiable for first order, can start with 200 pairs as trial.
  - "Margins unclear": We guarantee 25% minimum, up to 40% on hero SKUs.
